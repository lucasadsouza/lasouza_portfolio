About Me

I am an youth what spends his days running back and forth between school and courses. In addition to being a writer, I am primarily a lover of natural beauty and human complexity.

I believe in a life in which art takes us to travel in other worlds, other lives and dimensions. I try every day to improve my drawing skills to become a better artist with that goal in mind: to travel with art.

I started drawing at 6 years old, and... I needed to get myself up on my own because I never had any help. I fought alone with my drawings to evolve day by day, I fell a lot, but my love for drawing kept me all this time. Well... I like being an artist.
